Smart Legal Contracts

This demonstration illustrates how a legal agreement (such as a shipping contract) can be automated with the use of trusted data from the IBM Blockchain Platform.

The Clause platform provides tooling to manage commercial contracts that adhere to the open-source standard for smart legal contracts.

Avocados on the Blockchain

Greater traceability and transparency in the sourcing, production, and distribution value chain will reduce costs, risks, and inefficiencies. Further, the terms and conditions, standards and rules associated with a supply chain extend the paper trail.

A Smart ClauseTM enables contract automation to reduce your admin effort but still retains the legal strength of your agreements.

Follow the instructions below to create your agreement with Clause, then bind it to a supply-chain network running on IBM Blockchain Platform. You will submit sensors readings for storage on the blockchain and see the resulting balance updates for two supply chain network participants.

All done? So what next?
